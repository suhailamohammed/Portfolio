About Me

Hi, Suhaila here !

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh. I opted for this aforementioned degree since I believe that technology is an integral part of our lives. We are currently living in the midst of a digital era,therefore, most industries rely on data and software programmes. Also it is important to mention that there are ample amount of opportunities available following this career path due to the surge in the demand of technology usage. My major interest is getting involved in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, which is currently high in demand to bring about technological advancements in the future.

One of my favourite leisure time activities include reading books. Books are my escape from the daily hectic schedule. I always carry atleast a book along wherever I go to ease down my nerves. Reading books help to broaden my insights from what I usually observe.

And with that traveling becomes my another hobby as I believe travelling is the best way to explore oneself. It pushes you out of your comfort zone to realize how capable you are to face the outside world. I always crave for newer experiences and traveling makes that happen. You go to new places, do not understand the local language, and depend on sign language. And when you return home, you bring sweet memories of your incredible adventure back with you.
